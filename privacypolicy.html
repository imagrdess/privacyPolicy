We Collect No Personal Information Using Nuit Blanche Walking Tour

We do not collect, save, transfer, or have access to any of your personal data from your use of Nuit Blanche Walking Tour for Android or Nuit Blanche Walking Tour for iOS.    Your location data is only accessed while the appis in use and used for displaying your distance to various events and your location on the map. All processing of the location data is done on device and never stored, transferred or shared.
